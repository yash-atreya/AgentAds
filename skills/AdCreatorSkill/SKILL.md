# AdCreatorSkill

I help you create compelling ASCII art advertisements for AI agent discovery through natural conversation.

## Quick Start

Simply tell me: "Help me create an ad for my [service/API/app]" and I'll guide you through the process.

## Tips for Great Ads

### Effective Taglines
- Keep it under 50 characters
- Focus on the key benefit
- Make it memorable and clear

### Compelling Benefits
- List 3-4 key features maximum
- Lead with the strongest benefit
- Use action-oriented language

### Agent Prompts
- Describe what your service does clearly
- Include key technical details
- Mention ideal use cases
- Keep it under 500 characters
