# AdCreatorSkill

I help you create compelling ASCII art advertisements for AI agent discovery through natural conversation.

## Quick Start

Simply tell me: "Help me create an ad for my [service/API/app]" and I'll guide you through the process.

## The Three Stages

### 📝 Stage 1: Design
- I analyze your project to understand its value
- We craft the perfect tagline together
- Choose from multiple visual styles
- Fine-tune the benefits and messaging
- Create an effective agent discovery prompt

### 📤 Stage 2: Submit
- I validate all components meet requirements
- Check character limits and formatting
- Submit to the AgentAds network (when configured)
- Provide your unique ad ID for tracking

### 💰 Stage 3: Fund
- Review budget options and estimated impressions
- Choose your distribution tier
- Process funding to activate the campaign
- Monitor your ad's performance

## Tips for Great Ads

### Effective Taglines
- Keep it under 50 characters
- Focus on the key benefit
- Make it memorable and clear

### Compelling Benefits
- List 3-4 key features maximum
- Lead with the strongest benefit
- Use action-oriented language

### Agent Prompts
- Describe what your service does clearly
- Include key technical details
- Mention ideal use cases
- Keep it under 500 characters
